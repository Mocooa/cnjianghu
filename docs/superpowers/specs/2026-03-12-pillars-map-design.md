# cnjianghu Pillars Map Design

## Goal
Create a single bilingual PNG that turns the first-layer `pillar -> dossier` architecture into a readable, shareable artifact.

## Chosen Direction
Use a structured infographic rather than a poster-tree or atlas spread.

Rationale:
- The current priority is completeness and readability, not expressive visual experimentation.
- The image must hold all six pillars and their first-layer dossier pools in one view.
- The structure should feel archival and curated, while remaining practical for review.

## Composition
- Landscape canvas
- One title band across the top
- Six pillar cards in a `3 x 2` grid
- Each card contains:
  - English pillar title
  - Chinese pillar title
  - A short role line
  - Two compact dossier columns with bilingual labels

## Visual Language
- Clean, archival information design
- Ink background with restrained accent colors per pillar
- Chinese-first reading support with English parity
- Dense but deliberate spacing so the piece reads like an index page, not a slide
