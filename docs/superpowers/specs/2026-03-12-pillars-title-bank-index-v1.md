## Production Layer Rules

- Audience remains foreigners trying to understand China
- Titles should prioritize:
  - click-through
  - understanding
  - continued exploration
  - shareability
- Working title formats currently used:
  - `Quick Bites`
  - `Misread / Actually`
  - `Scene Decoder`
  - `Deep Dives`
  - `Glossary-led`

## Tagging Rules

- `Launch first`
  - Applied to titles that sit inside each file's strongest dossier clusters, based on the existing `Editorial Notes` priority list.
- `Evergreen`
  - Applied to titles with stable explanatory value, especially `Misread / Actually`, `Deep Dives`, and `Glossary-led`, plus most non-trend-bound `Quick Bites` and `Scene Decoder` titles.
- `High shareability`
  - Applied primarily to `Quick Bites`, `Misread / Actually`, and `Scene Decoder`, where title structure is already optimized for curiosity and forwarding.
- `Needs glossary support`
  - Applied to all `Glossary-led` titles, titles with untranslated or semi-translated terms, and dossiers whose ideas are likely to need term scaffolding for foreign readers.
