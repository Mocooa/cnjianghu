## What it is

马面裙 is a skirt structure recognizable for its flat front and back panels with pleated sides. In recent years it became one of the most publicly visible garments in the Hanfu revival.

## Why people talk about it

The term matters because it sits at the meeting point of:

- historical dress interest
- everyday wear adaptation
- social-media styling
- debates over tradition and trend

## What outsiders often miss

Mamianqun is not simply a costume item. Its recent visibility comes partly from the fact that it can travel more easily than a full historical outfit into modern public dress.

## Why it matters

If you want to understand why Hanfu suddenly feels more mainstream, 马面裙 is one of the clearest garments to look at.
