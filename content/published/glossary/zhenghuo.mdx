## What kind of word this is

整活 is internet slang, but it is not just a funny label. It usually implies intention, performance, and an awareness of audience.

## What people usually mean

When someone says **又开始整活了**, they mean a person is not simply acting. They are making a scene, escalating a joke, inventing a bit, or deliberately producing content out of a situation.

## Where it appears

- group chats
- livestreams
- short-video culture
- fan communities
- any situation where performance and reaction feed each other

## What outsiders often miss

整活 is not just "messing around." It often involves effort, timing, and a sense of play for others to witness. The social reaction is part of the act.

## Why it matters

整活 helps explain why so much Chinese internet culture feels participatory. Humor is not only delivered to an audience; it is built with an audience in mind.
