## What it is

企业微信, branded in English as **WeCom**, is Tencent's workplace communication product. It overlaps with chat and collaboration tools, but it also sits close to the broader WeChat ecosystem.

## What people usually mean

When people say they will "add you on 企业微信," they usually mean a work relationship rather than a purely private one. The term can signal:

- internal office coordination
- client communication
- sales follow-up
- school or institution messaging

## What makes it different

WeCom matters because work communication in China often does not sit in a completely separate digital world. It can stay adjacent to the same habits, contacts, and platform logic people already use in WeChat.

## What outsiders often miss

WeCom is not just "China's Slack." It belongs to a platform environment where work, service, and personal communication are often closer together than foreign users expect.

## Why it matters

If WeChat helps explain Chinese social infrastructure, WeCom helps explain why workplace communication in China can feel unusually continuous with the rest of digital life.
