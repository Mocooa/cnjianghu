## What this page is

`Multi-Step Task` works best here as a **concept note**. In Chinese AI conversation, 多步任务 is not just a neutral benchmark phrase. It has become a practical way of asking whether a system can move from one answer to something closer to real completion.

## What people usually mean

When product teams or users talk about 多步任务, they are often asking whether an AI system can:

- understand a compound goal
- break it into stages
- keep track of intermediate state
- call tools or gather information
- return something usable at the end

So the phrase points less to one formal benchmark than to a broader expectation about usefulness.

## What outsiders often miss

This is not only a technical-evaluation phrase. In the Chinese AI story, 多步任务 matters because it connects model capability to product adoption. If an AI can finish more of the workflow, it becomes easier to imagine it inside everyday work and consumer tools.

## What we should not overclaim

There is no single universal China-only benchmark hidden behind 多步任务. Different products, labs, and platforms may mean slightly different things by it. But as a public concept, it captures a real shift in attention from one-turn cleverness toward workflow completion.
