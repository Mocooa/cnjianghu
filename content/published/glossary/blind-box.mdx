## What it is

盲盒 is a sealed product format where the exact figure or design is unknown until opening.

## What people are buying

People are not only buying the object inside. They are also buying:

- surprise
- serial completion
- repeat purchase tension
- the possibility of a rare pull

## What outsiders often miss

Blind box culture is not just childish impulse buying. In China it became a broader emotional and aesthetic collecting system, especially through brands like Pop Mart.

## Why it matters

If you want to understand why collectible culture in China feels so intense, 盲盒 is one of the core product formats behind it.
