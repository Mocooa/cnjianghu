## What kind of word this is

老铁 is an address term. In livestream culture it works less like precise description and more like emotional positioning.

## What people usually mean

When a streamer says **老铁们**, the point is usually to create:

- rough warmth
- immediacy
- loyalty
- a feeling that host and audience are on the same side

It can sound earthy, familiar, and performatively sincere.

## Where it appears

- livestream rooms
- short-video commerce
- bantering online speech
- settings where audience closeness needs to be built fast

## What outsiders often miss

老铁 is not a neutral synonym for "friend." In livestream use, it is part of the room atmosphere. It helps turn viewers into a crowd with emotional texture rather than a generic audience.

## Why it matters

老铁 helps explain why Chinese livestreaming often feels socially thick. Address terms themselves do part of the trust-building work.
