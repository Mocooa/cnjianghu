## What people usually mean

家常菜 refers to the everyday dishes people associate with ordinary home eating. The point is not that they are fancy, difficult, or especially photogenic. The point is that they are familiar, repeatable, and tied to domestic routine.

## What kind of food it points to

家常菜 often suggests dishes that are:

- practical to cook often
- recognizable inside a family
- adjusted by habit rather than strict recipe
- comforting because they are ordinary

That is why a dish can be very simple and still feel deeply specific as 家常菜.

## What outsiders often miss

家常菜 is not just "Chinese home cooking" as a broad cuisine label. It carries a sense of the normal, the durable, and the lived-in. Restaurant food may represent a place, but 家常菜 often represents a household.

## Why it matters

If foreigners want to understand why Chinese food memory is often attached to one plain-looking dish rather than to generic "Chinese cuisine," 家常菜 is one of the most useful terms to know.
