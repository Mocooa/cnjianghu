## What it is

接龙 usually means one person starts a numbered sequence in a group chat and everyone continues it in order.

## What it does

It is a lightweight coordination tool for:

- meal orders
- attendance
- school notices
- delivery pickup
- volunteer sign-ups
- any situation where the group needs visible confirmation

Because everyone replies in sequence, responsibility becomes easier to see.

## Where it appears

- WeChat parent groups
- office groups
- residential-compound groups
- neighborhood coordination

## What outsiders often miss

接龙 is not just a quirky chat habit. It shows how Chinese group life often turns messaging space into organizational infrastructure.

## Why it matters

If you want to understand how chat platforms in China carry real-world coordination, 接龙 is one of the clearest everyday examples.
