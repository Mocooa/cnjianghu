## What it is

换电 means replacing an EV battery with a charged one instead of waiting to charge the original pack in place.

## Why people care

In discussion about EV life in China, battery swap matters because it promises:

- speed
- routine convenience
- standardized service points
- a more infrastructure-like experience

## What outsiders often miss

Battery swap is not just a technical feature. In China it is often discussed as part of a broader question: how do you make electrified mobility feel ordinary at scale?

## Why it matters

If Chinese EV adoption feels unusually everyday rather than futuristic, 换电 is one of the mechanisms that helps explain that feeling.
