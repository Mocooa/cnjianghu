## What kind of word this is

## How it works

A 斗图 session usually starts organically. Someone drops a funny sticker. Someone else responds with a funnier one. The exchange escalates — reactions get more obscure, more exaggerated, more precisely targeted at the moment. The implicit "winner" is whoever deploys the most unexpected or devastatingly well-timed image. There are no formal rules, no scoring, and no prizes. The reward is the laughter and admiration of the group.

斗图 is most common in WeChat and QQ group chats among friends, classmates, or colleagues with a relaxed dynamic.
