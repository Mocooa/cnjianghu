## More than compromise

变通 is often translated as flexibility or adaptability, but the word usually suggests something more situational than a personality trait. It means adjusting method or form so that a path remains workable.

## What people usually mean

When people praise someone for 变通, they often mean:

- they read the situation before acting
- they did not get stuck on one rigid rule
- they found a workable way through constraint
- they adjusted form without abandoning the goal

## What outsiders often miss

变通 is not simply cutting corners. It can be admired because it shows judgment: knowing what should stay firm and what can be adjusted.

That is why in Chinese common sense, a workable solution can sometimes earn more respect than a pure but unusable one.

## Why it matters

If foreigners want to understand why Chinese problem-solving can look flexible rather than principle-first, 变通 is one of the key words.
