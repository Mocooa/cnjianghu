## What people usually mean

瑞兽 refers to creatures whose appearance signals good fortune, protection, harmony, or legitimate order. Depending on context, this can include dragons, qilin, phoenix-linked creatures, and other symbolic beings.

## Where it appears

- mythology and folklore
- architecture and ornament
- festival design
- branding and packaging
- fantasy art direction

## What outsiders often miss

An auspicious beast is not just a "monster with positive vibes." These creatures often carry long layers of ritual, political, and artistic meaning, which is why they travel so easily from temples to games to packaging.

## Why it matters

If you want to understand how Chinese mythic imagery stays alive in everyday design, 瑞兽 is one of the clearest entry points.
