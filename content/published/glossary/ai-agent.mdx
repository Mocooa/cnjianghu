## What people usually mean

In current Chinese product conversation, AI Agent usually points to a system that can do more than chat. It is expected to:

- break work into steps
- call tools or services
- maintain some task context
- help finish an outcome rather than only generate text

That is why 智能体 often feels more action-oriented than a general AI assistant.

## Why it matters in China

The term lands especially well in Chinese digital environments because people already live inside integrated app systems, payment systems, logistics systems, and workflow-heavy super-app habits. A tool that can actually complete tasks therefore makes immediate sense.

## What outsiders often miss

AI Agent in the China conversation is not only future talk or investor hype. It often appears in a practical product frame: can it save steps, finish work, and fit into existing digital routines?

## Why it matters

If foreigners want to understand why AI in China is often discussed through usefulness and task completion rather than pure novelty, AI Agent is one of the clearest entry terms.
