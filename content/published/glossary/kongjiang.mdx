## What it is

空降 in video culture usually means jumping straight to a marked timestamp instead of watching from the beginning. The word keeps the image of dropping in from above, which is why it feels more vivid than simply saying "skip ahead."

## What it does

In Chinese video culture, 空降 helps people:

- go straight to a famous scene
- revisit a known joke moment
- find a key emotional peak
- join a shared checkpoint in the comments or 弹幕

So it is not only a viewing shortcut. It is also a social way of arriving at the part everyone is already talking about.

## What outsiders often miss

空降 is not just random skipping. In Chinese online video culture it often assumes a collectively recognized moment worth landing on. That is why it fits especially well with 弹幕, highlight culture, and communal rewatching.

## Why it matters

If foreigners want to understand how Chinese viewers use timelines socially rather than purely individually, 空降 is one of the most useful small platform terms to know.
